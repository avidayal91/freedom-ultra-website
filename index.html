OUR MISSION

Every year since 2016 we, runners take a selfless inititative to bring change and support organizations who are working hard to help those who are in need.

We along with our cyclist friends, cover over 75 kms to raise awareness amongst individuals, families, communities and society to help the victim of wrong doings.

END INCEST

In this year's edition, we are supporting End Incest, an organization striving towards a world that ensures safe and secure homes free of incest abuse, through comprehensive awareness initiatives, collaborative planning, policy research, advocacy and community action.

Check www.endincest.org for more details.
